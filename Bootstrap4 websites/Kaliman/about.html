About Kaliman

Kaliman is a chihuahua mix. Currently 10 years old as of July 2019. He was born approximately in June-July 2009.

He's not trained and has not socialized that much with other dogs. While that's not ideal, he is very friendly to other people. Though, he'll have to smell you intensely first and then he'll probably lay on his back and wait for a belly rub.

With dogs his behavior is kind of unclear. At times he can be tense when a dog is on the other side of a fence, but he'll be timid when the dog is actually in front of him.
